Beware Of The Dark Side

Rug Pull is a lucrative scam in which a crypto developer promotes a new project - usually a new token - to investors and then drains the liquidity. E.g. Anubis DAO ($60 million).

Pump-and-dump is a manipulative scheme to raise the price of a token by making false, misleading or exaggerated statements. Retail is inexperienced in the way blockchain and crypto work.

These are some characteristics shared between NFTs and ponzi schemes, which are proven to crumble and take you down if you do not "get in on the ground floor". Azuki ($100+ million).

Social Engineering Audits

There are on-chain security audits of smart contracts code, but there are no off-chain audits that test how project team members, especially discord mods, react to social engineering attempts. Mute DAO offers a unique service where a project can request our social engineering service. We will use different techniques and test how the team reacts to a direct approach (DM) from a stranger. Our report includes a rating system for each individual, backed by data (e.g. click on a malicious link) and is shared with the founder (requester).
