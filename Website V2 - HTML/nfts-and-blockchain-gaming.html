NFTs and Blockchain Gaming

Today, almost every game in the blockchain gaming industry is driven by NFTs. Unlike traditional in-game purchases, where these purchases are confined to a single gaming world, NFTs provide interoperable ownership that can be represented in other games. With blockchain technology, gamers can now save in-game purchases, sell them to other gamers, or use them in other games.    Another benefit of NFTs in blockchain gaming is through provable scarcity. Imagine you put in hours into a game and unlocked an exclusive achievement. With NFTs, you can actually prove the rarity and authenticity of your achievement, whereas, in traditional games, your achievements don’t mean a thing outside of the game.    

This brings us to one of the most important features of NFTs, Interoperability. While traditional games are known to offer siloed experiences, blockchain games are completely interoperable, thanks to NFTs. Since blockchain games are decentralized and exist on independent blockchains, game assets represented by NFTs can be designed to hold their value across different games and environments.
