How NFTs are Evolving the Way We Play Games

NFTs ushered in a new era of digital ownership, mainly in areas like digital art, fashion, and, most successfully, gaming, where virtual ownership is highly regarded. While a majority of the hype has been surrounding digital art projects like Crypto Punks, Bored Ape Yacht Club, and NBA Topshot; however, games like Axie Infinity, MetaBlaze, Sandbox, and more have also been shaking up the industry.

NFTs have empowered creators to express themselves through various mediums and allow them to rightfully monetize and value their digital content, whether it is music, videos, memes, art, or in-game items, without having to go through the challenges that a modern-day creator or developer has to go through. Especially when the global gaming industry is structured in such a way that game developers and studios can only benefit through unlockables that players spend money on. League of Legends, Dota, Apex Legends, and Fortnite are some of the most played games in the world. Interestingly, all of them are free to play.
